    Press The Coin And Let It Bloom

    This HTML demo mirrors the original coin’s thick 3D stack, face swap, and 180 degree flip timing, then adds a hold-to-charge interaction so you can judge whether the glowing, swelling energy feels worth bringing back into the app.

    Tap
    A quick click flips the coin between its two faces with a compressed shadow, like the React component.
    Hold
    Holding charges the glow, expands the aura and ring, and triggers a burst plus a random menu when the meter peaks.
    Quick click to flip. Hold to charge.
